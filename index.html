All About Scooby Doo

"Scooby Dooby Doo, where are you? We got some work to do now! Scooby Dooby Doo, where are you? We need some help from you now!"

Fun Facts:

  • His full name is Scoobert-Doo, inspired by a line from Frank Sinatra's Strangers in the Night, "doo-be-doo-be-doo".
  • He is a Great Dane dog.
  • Scooby's excessive use of the letter “R” is called rhotacism.
